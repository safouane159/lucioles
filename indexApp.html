How can i connect my ESP32 to our Website?

Step1 : Click "get my key"

Step2 : you will receive an email to your account with your "key"

Step3 : download the arduino code from this repo :

Step4 : inject the code to your ESP32 ( Change wifiMulti.addAP("", "") with your wifi id and password )

Step5 : open http://your-local-ip-address/ (Warning: you need to set up the spiffs ),you will be redirected to a page where you can add your KEY

Step6 : enter the key you received in your Gmail

Step7 : your ESP32 is connected to our website, you can see the data in the chart
